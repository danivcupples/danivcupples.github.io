About Me

I'm a (mostly) self-taught front-end web developer. Currently I'm most comfortable with HTML and CSS, but my JavaScript proficiency improves daily. I have used Bootstrap and jQuery to varying degrees of success, but hope to use them more and more and I continue to work toward a greater mastery of coding.

I live in Charlotte, North Carolina with my family, which consists of my husband Houston, our daughter Wendy, and our dog Toby. I currently work for Safe-Card ID services, and am working on redesigning our website and implementing Magento Community Edition as our ecommerce platform.

My life runs on coffee. I also like books, bourbon, beer, good food, and good stationery.
